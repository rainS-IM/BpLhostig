Court Clash Basketball

Support This Game

HOW TO PLAY: 

Red player:
A/D to lean
W to shoot
----------
Blue player:
Left/Right to lean
Up to shoot
----------
Or, use the input buttons (touchscreen only)!
----------
First to 9 wins!

CREDITS: 

Made in Unity
Using CameraShakeFX, UI_InteractSounds, MusicStingersPack, TextMeshPro, fonts from TopDownEngine
Music from AlgorithmicTrap @ https://algorithmictrap.com/
Inspired by Basket Random and Getaway Shootout
